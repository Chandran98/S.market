What personal data is collected?

In connection with the use of the S.Mart app and your purchases, the following data may be processed:

  • User account data (e.g., username, e-mail address)
  • Purchase data (e.g., object of purchase, amount)
  • Transaction data (e.g., transaction ID, date, card issuer, last four digits of PAN, terminal ID)
  • Loyalty program data (e.g., member ID, bonus points)
  • Device and meta data (e.g., IP address, device model, OS version)
  • Location data (only with user opt-in)
  • Push notification tokens (only with user opt-in)
  • Message content if you contact us

Also, the S.Mart ceiling cameras generate visuals for brief, anonymized analysis purposes. These recordings are immediately obfuscated and cannot be traced back to individuals.

For what purpose and on what legal basis is the processing carried out?

  • Shopping experience: For access, purchases, and billing. Legal basis: GDPR Art. 6(1)(b) and (f).
  • Device data: To provide secure and stable app usage. Legal basis: GDPR Art. 6(1)(f).
  • Consent-based features: Location and push tokens. Legal basis: GDPR Art. 6(1)(a).
  • Analytics: Anonymous and aggregated data. Legal basis: GDPR Art. 6(1)(f).
  • Inquiries: Support via email or contact form. Legal basis: GDPR Art. 6(1)(b).
  • Legal obligations and claims: Sharing data with public bodies if required. Legal basis: GDPR Art. 6(1)(c) and (f).

With whom do we share your data?

Payfree GmbH – RFID technology provider (Germany).
Adyen N.V. – Payment service provider (Netherlands).
SAP SE – Technology provider for app and store systems.

Some processing may occur outside the EEA under EU Standard Contractual Clauses. Contact us for more details.

How long do we store personal data?

Data is stored as long as necessary for its purpose and based on legal retention periods (6–8 years). When no longer needed, it is anonymized or securely deleted.
